About

Technical work delivered with preparation and follow-through.

Lapointa's IT Service is a Dayton-based independent technical-services provider serving small businesses, technology vendors, field-service companies, infrastructure teams, and remote clients.

The approach

Defined outcomes. Professional execution.

Good technical service is more than completing a task. It means understanding the scope, preparing for the environment, communicating throughout the work, validating the result, and leaving useful documentation behind.

Services are limited to verified experience, available tools, appropriate safety qualifications, and customer-approved procedures. Regulated, licensed, high-voltage, security, or specialized work should only be completed by properly qualified and insured professionals.
